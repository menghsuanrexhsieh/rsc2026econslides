## About the course

The RSC Economics Experience is an intensive summer course meant to build real
economic intuition in a short window. We move from **microeconomics** (how people
and firms make decisions) to **macroeconomics** (how those decisions add up across
an economy), and close with **applied policy** questions you can reason about on
your own.

No prior economics is assumed — only curiosity and comfort with basic algebra.
